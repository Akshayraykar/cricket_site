❓ IPL 2026 Frequently Asked Questions
When does IPL 2026 start and end? +
IPL 2026 starts on March 28, 2026, with RCB vs SRH at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru at 7:30 PM IST. The tournament runs for 65 days with the Grand Final on May 31, 2026 also in Bengaluru.
How many matches are there in IPL 2026? +
IPL 2026 features 84 matches — 10 more than the previous season. This increase is due to a revised double round-robin format where teams play more games. There will be 74 league matches, plus 4 playoff matches including the Grand Final.
Who are the defending champions of IPL 2026? +
Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) are the defending champions, having won their maiden IPL title in 2025 after defeating Punjab Kings in the final. Rajat Patidar leads RCB into the 2026 season.
Which team has won the most IPL titles? +
Mumbai Indians (MI) and Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are joint record holders with 5 IPL titles each. KKR have won 3, while GT have won 2, and SRH, RR, and RCB have won 1 each.
Where can I watch IPL 2026 live for free? +
IPL 2026 will be available free on JioCinema for Jio users in India. Star Sports 1, 2, 3 HD will broadcast on TV. For online streaming, Disney+ Hotstar requires a subscription.
What is the IPL 2026 team budget for auctions? +
Each IPL 2026 franchise was allotted a total budget of ₹125 crore to build a squad of 18–25 players. The combined auction spend across all 10 teams was ₹237.55 crore. The minimum player base price was ₹30 lakh and maximum ₹2 crore.
